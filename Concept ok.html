The Concept

Because we're drowning in plastic

We Are MercaVrac

Every time we go grocery shopping, we're forced to buy packaging that we throw away as soon as we get home.

The result? On average, each French citizen discards 141 kg of waste per year. Globally, plastic waste amounts to 300 million tons annually.

And this is just the beginning: if we keep going at this rate, plastic production is expected to triple by 2060...

Ok, glass… But why returnable?

Explain what we are going :

Glass is often seen as a great alternative since it has a low impact on biodiversity and a high recycling rate (88%). However, recycling glass requires a lot of energy and CO₂ emissions for both melting and transportation.

Think about it: transporting waste to a specialized recycling center (if one even exists), melting glass at 1,200°C, consuming massive amounts of gas, and then manufacturing new bottles—often with additional raw materials. Surely, there’s a more efficient way?

Recycling has become an easy excuse for disposable packaging: "I throw it away, but it’s okay because it’s recycled."The real issue isn’t just recycling—it’s the act of throwing away that needs to be reconsidered.

The deposit system, the best alternative

What is the Deposit System?

The deposit system is a simple and effective solution that allows packaging to be reused instead of thrown away. Instead of buying a product in single-use packaging, you receive it in a returnable container, which we collect, clean, and reuse.

How Does It Work?

Explain what we are going :

1. You order your bulk products in returnable glass jars.

2. You enjoy fresh, local, and plastic-free products.

3. During your next delivery, our driver picks up your empty jars directly from your home.

4. We clean and reuse them for a new life cycle.

A Virtuous Circle for the Environment

A life with less waste

Thanks to the deposit system and bulk sales, we significantly reduce waste, limit plastic use, and preserve natural resources. No need to worry about returning your containers—we take care of everything for you!
