About Us

Hi ! We are Louise, Audrey and Valentine, three friends passionate about ecology and innovation. All three students at Sup de Pub, we had a real epiphany during a humanitarian trip to South America. Traveling along the coasts and through remote villages, we were struck by the omnipresence of plastic: beaches littered with waste, residents forced to burn their packaging due to a lack of recycling solutions... It was then that we realized the urgency of taking action.

Back in Madrid, where we continued our studies in communications and entrepreneurship, we decided to move from awareness to action. So, in 2025, we created MercaVrac, driven by our shared desire to make responsible consumption accessible to all, without constraints. Through our platform, we aim to prove that a zero-waste lifestyle can be simple, practical, and modern.

With MercaVrac, we hope to bring as many Madrilenians as possible on board this sustainable adventure.



Testimonials from the founders of MercaVrac


Audrey

Awareness from childhood "When I was little, I spent my summers with my grandparents in the countryside, where everything was homemade and plastic packaging was almost nonexistent. My grandmother bought her vegetables in bulk at the market and reused the same jars for years. As I grew up, I realized how much our consumption habits had changed and the cost to the planet. With MercaVrac, I want to rediscover this simplicity and prove that consuming without plastic is possible, even in the city."



Louise

The Supermarket Shopping Trigger “Shopping has always been a source of frustration for me. Seeing all this over-packaged food and single-use plastic bags seemed absurd, even though we now know the damage it causes. But even when I wanted to consume better, I was often held back by the lack of practical options. I understood that a simpler and more effective solution was needed to integrate bulk shopping into my daily life.” With MercaVrac, we wanted to break down these barriers and show that we can consume differently, without the hassle.



Valentine

Valentine – The Impact of a Humanitarian Trip “During our humanitarian trip to Asia, I was shocked by the mountains of plastic waste accumulating on the beaches and in villages. There, I saw families living surrounded by waste due to the lack of an effective recycling system. This trip was a real shock. Upon my return, I changed my consumption habits and wanted to go further: create a sustainable alternative accessible to all. MercaVrac is our response to this environmental emergency.”


MercaVrac was born from our shared commitment and our personal experiences.

Our goal?


To offer an accessible and practical alternative for more responsible consumption, in the heart of Madrid.
