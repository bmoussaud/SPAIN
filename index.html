Welcome to MercaVrac

Your sustainable bulk shopping destination. We deliver fresh, local products in reusable containers right to your door. is an innovative platform designed to make bulk shopping easier and more accessible. We offer a home and locker delivery service for bulk products in reusable containers, which are picked up and refilled with each new order. Our goal is to centralize bulk shopping, eliminating the need to visit multiple stores for different products. We aim to provide a wide variety of sustainable, plastic-free products, making eco-friendly consumption convenient for everyone.
