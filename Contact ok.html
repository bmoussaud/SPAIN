This site is a result of the work of the students (include your names) from the Basic Web Design course of the Bachelor of Multimedia and Graphic design at the Universidad de Diseño, Innovación y Tecnología (UDIT) in Madrid, Spain, from the 2024/2025 academic year. The subject, taught by Professor Juan Romero-Luis, is part of the project "ECOSISTEMA DE ENTORNOS DE CONOCIMIENTO ABIERTO (ECO2)".

The project forms a part of the strategic line by GID COMTEDEA, which aims to establish open learning spaces applicable to various subjects. The Classroom Ciberimaginario hosts contents and open educational practices (OEP) that enrich the learning experiences of students across various disciplines, deepening their understanding of subject-specific aspects and bringing them closer to real-world professional scenarios.

In the academic year 2022/23, the "Open Knowledge Environment and Educational Practices" platform was introduced. Developed under the open knowledge paradigm, it saw members of the GID publishing open educational resources.

This project broadens the scope of the "Open Educational Knowledge and Practices Environment", hosted on Classroom Ciberimaginario, thereby creating an "Open Educational Ecosystem".


It is also included as a resource in the Climate Warriors initiative.
