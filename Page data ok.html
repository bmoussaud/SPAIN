Regulations and Measures In Place

Faced with the growing environmental crisis, Madrid is stepping up its efforts to combat plastic pollution. The city is determined to lead by example, showing that change is possible through concrete action and collective willpower.

Spain Policies

Spain has implemented several policies to curb plastic consumption : Ban on single-use plastic items, including cutlery, plates, straws, and cotton buds. 0.45/kg € tax on non-recycled plastic, effective January 2023. Restrictions on plastic packaging, especially for food products like fruits and vegetables. Target of 50% plastic packaging recycling by 2025.

Madrid, like the rest of Spain, is actively working to reduce its plastic footprint. With stricter regulations, innovation in sustainable alternatives, and a growing environmental consciousness, the city is on the path to a greener future.
